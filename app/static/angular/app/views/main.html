Welcome to Titan Search

Use our search feature to look for Wikipedia articles and we'll make your life easier by automatically pulling the latest news and social media posts that are relevant to the Wiki page you choose. Search on!

Where we get our information from


Wikipedia

Wikipedia is the world's largest and most popular free encyclopedia. We enable you to search a large range of topics from the English Wikipedia which consists of over 4.6 million articles.

News

News is one of the fastest growing sectors of the Internet. We index more than 10 thousand articles to find the latest news that relates to your search.

Twitter

Twitter is the world's source for the latest happenings all over the globe. Our search over Twitter feeds make surethat you are never of the loop from what's happening right now.
